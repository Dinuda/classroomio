# Customize your Organization

ClassroomIO empowers every organization with the ability to tailor their experience to their unique needs.
To customize your organization, navigate to **Settings** > **Organization** from your dashboard.

<Image src={CustomizeOrg} alt="ClassroomIO Orgainzation settings page" quality="100" />

## Update Organization name

Easily update your organization's name by modifying the designated field in the Organization Profile section of the Settings page, accessible through **Settings** > **Organization**.
In the Orgainzation Profile section you can update your organization name and also update your organization profile image

## Customise Domain

Upon onboarding, each organization in ClassroomIO is assigned a custom Domain URL. This URL directs users to the organization's customizable landing page, making it easy for audiences to access published courses, FAQs, and other relevant information tailored to your organization's preferences. To customize your organization's domain, click on "Edit Domain" and update the URL field with your preferred choice.

## Set brand color

We understand that every organization has a preferred color that suits their brand. With just a single click, we offer organizations the flexibility to choose a theme for their brand from a variety of colors like **blue**, **green**, **orange**, and more.
